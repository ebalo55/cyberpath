## Overview

The **OffSec macOS Researcher (OSMR)** certification is designed for individuals and teams to proactively identify
threats and protect organizational infrastructure. This certification validates your expertise in macOS security and
demonstrates your ability to analyze and exploit complex vulnerabilities.

The certification is obtained through the **Advanced macOS Control Bypasses (EXP-312)** course, which provides a
comprehensive understanding of macOS security and its inner workings.

## Course Details

The EXP-312 course emphasizes hands-on experience in identifying and exploiting logic-based vulnerabilities within
macOS, learning to bypass security mechanisms, and escalating privileges to gain root access.

### Topics Covered:

- macOS security mechanisms and bypass techniques.
- Exploitation of logic-based vulnerabilities.
- Privilege escalation to root access.
- Comprehensive analysis of macOS security.

## Exam Structure

The OSMR certification is awarded upon successful completion of the online training course and passing the associated
exam.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of the EXP-312 course.
- Successful exploitation of macOS vulnerabilities.
- Demonstration of root access on macOS systems.

## Who Should Take OSMR?

The OSMR is ideal for:

- macOS security specialists.
- Security researchers focusing on macOS.
- Penetration testers looking to expand their expertise.
