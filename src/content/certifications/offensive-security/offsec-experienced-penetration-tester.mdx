The **Offensive Security Experienced Penetration Tester (OSEP)** certification is designed for professionals who want
to advance their penetration testing skills. Building on the foundational skills from PEN-200, the PEN-300 course
focuses on advanced techniques against hardened targets.

## Course Details

The OSEP certification is tied to the **PEN-300** course, which emphasizes hands-on experience and practical application
of advanced penetration testing techniques.

### Topics Covered:

- Advanced exploitation techniques.
- Custom exploit development.
- Evasion tactics against modern security defenses.
- Real-time attack simulations against mature security infrastructures.

## Exam Structure

The OSEP certification is awarded upon successful completion of a challenging exam that tests the candidate's ability to
apply advanced penetration testing skills in a controlled environment.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Practical exam with real-world scenarios.
- Development and documentation of custom exploits.
- Proof of successful compromises and bypasses.

## Who Should Take OSEP?

The OSEP is ideal for:

- Experienced penetration testers.
- Security professionals aiming to enhance their offensive capabilities.
- Red team operators looking to tackle advanced security challenges.
