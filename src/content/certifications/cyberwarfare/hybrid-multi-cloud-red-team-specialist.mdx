## Overview

The **Certified Hybrid Multi-Cloud Red Team Specialist (CHMRTS)** certification is designed for cybersecurity
professionals who want to specialize in conducting red team operations across hybrid multi-cloud environments. This
certification provides the skills necessary to assess and exploit vulnerabilities in major cloud platforms such as AWS,
Azure, and Google Cloud.

## Course Details

The CHMRTS certification is structured into four comprehensive sections, each focusing on specific aspects of red
teaming in hybrid multi-cloud environments.

### Topics Covered:

- **Attacking - AWS Environment**: Techniques for exploiting vulnerabilities within AWS services.
- **Attacking - Azure Environment**: Strategies for conducting assessments in Microsoft Azure.
- **Attacking - GCP Environment**: Approaches for identifying risks in Google Cloud Platform.
- **Attacking - Hybrid Multi-Cloud Environment**: Best practices for integrating red team operations across various
  cloud infrastructures.

### Cyber Range Labs Highlights:

Participants will have access to cyber range labs that simulate real-world scenarios, allowing candidates to practice
their skills in a controlled environment.

## Exam Structure

The CHMRTS certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that assesses the candidate's ability
to perform red team operations across hybrid multi-cloud platforms.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Participation in hands-on labs that simulate real-world hybrid cloud environments.

## Who Should Take CHMRTS?

The CHMRTS certification is ideal for:

- Cybersecurity professionals looking to enhance their skills in cloud security.
- Red teamers wanting to specialize in hybrid multi-cloud environments.
- Penetration testers seeking to understand the unique challenges posed by multi-cloud infrastructures.
