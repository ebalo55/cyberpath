## Overview

The **Certified Cyber Defence Analyst (CCDA)** certification is tailored for individuals seeking to establish a strong
foundation in cyber defense. This certification equips candidates with the skills necessary to detect, investigate, and
respond to various cyber threats, including phishing and network intrusions.

The certification validates an individual’s ability to:

- Conduct thorough investigations of suspected cyber threats.
- Utilize defensive solutions such as SIEM and incident response tools.
- Understand and implement an organization’s cyber defense strategy.

## Course Details

The CCDA certification comprises five comprehensive sections, each focusing on key areas of cyber defense.

### Topics Covered:

- **Introduction to Cyber Defence**: Fundamentals of cyber defense principles and practices.
- **Phishing Threats Investigation and Analysis**: Techniques for identifying and mitigating phishing attacks.
- **Web-Based Intrusions**: Investigative strategies for analyzing web-based threats.
- **Network Intrusions**: Methods for uncovering and analyzing network-based attacks.
- **Host-Based Intrusions**: Techniques for investigating host-centric threats.

## Exam Structure

The CCDA certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that includes simulated investigative
challenges. Candidates are allowed two attempts to pass the exam.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Participation in hands-on lab exercises with access to a simulated blue team environment.

## Who Should Take CCDA?

The CCDA certification is ideal for:

- Beginners interested in pursuing a career in cyber defense.
- Individuals looking to enhance their skills in threat detection and incident response.
- Cybersecurity managers aiming to build foundational knowledge within their teams.
