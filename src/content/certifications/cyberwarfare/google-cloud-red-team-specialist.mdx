## Overview

The **Certified Google Cloud Red Team Specialist (CGRTS)** certification is designed for cybersecurity professionals who
want to specialize in red teaming within Google Cloud environments. This certification provides the skills necessary to
conduct effective penetration tests and security assessments using Google Cloud Platform (GCP) tools and methodologies.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Understand Google Cloud architecture and security features.
- Execute red team operations effectively in a cloud environment.
- Identify and exploit vulnerabilities specific to Google Cloud services.

## Course Details

The CGRTS certification consists of three comprehensive sections, each focusing on critical aspects of red teaming in
Google Cloud.

### Topics Covered:

- **Google Cloud & Red Team Fundamentals**:
    - Overview of Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Google Workspace.
    - Understanding Google Cloud authentication and access controls.
    - Introduction to red team methodologies.

- **Blue Team Operations in Google Cloud Environment**:
    - Implementing security controls.
    - Utilizing logging and monitoring tools.
    - Leveraging the Security Command Center for threat detection.

- **Red Team Operations in Google Cloud Environment**:
    - Conducting open source information gathering (OSINT).
    - Techniques for gaining initial access and performing internal reconnaissance.
    - Strategies for privilege escalation, maintaining access, and lateral movement.
    - Hunting for credentials and achieving operational objectives.

### Cyber Range Labs Highlights:

Participants will have access to cyber range labs that simulate real-world scenarios, allowing candidates to practice
their skills in a controlled environment.

## Exam Structure

The CGRTS certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that assesses the candidate's ability
to perform red team operations within Google Cloud environments.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Participation in hands-on labs that simulate real-world cloud environments.

## Who Should Take CGRTS?

The CGRTS certification is ideal for:

- Intermediate to advanced cybersecurity professionals looking to enhance their skills in cloud security.
- Red teamers wanting to specialize in operations within Google Cloud environments.
- Penetration testers seeking to understand the unique challenges posed by cloud infrastructures.
