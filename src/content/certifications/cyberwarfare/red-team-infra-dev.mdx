## Overview

The **Certified Red Team Infra Developer (CRT-ID)** certification is tailored for intermediate to advanced professionals
in the red teaming and penetration testing fields. This certification focuses on developing and implementing
infrastructure for effective red team operations, enabling candidates to simulate sophisticated cyber attacks.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Design and deploy secure red team infrastructures.
- Perform adversarial techniques, including credential capture and MFA bypass.
- Utilize both cloud and on-premise resources for traffic redirection.

## Course Details

The CRT-ID certification includes a comprehensive course that combines theoretical knowledge with practical exercises,
delivered through on-demand lectures and hands-on activities.

### Topics Covered:

- **OPSEC Safe Infrastructure**: Best practices for secure red team operations.
- **Initial Access Operations**: Techniques for gaining initial access to target environments.
- **Adversary in the Middle Attack (AiTM)**: Methods for bypassing multi-factor authentication.
- **Cloud & On-Premise Setup**: Configuring resources for effective traffic management.

## Exam Structure

The CRT-ID certification is awarded upon successful completion of a task-based practical exam that evaluates the
candidate's understanding of red team infrastructure development. A passing score of **75%** is required to earn the
digital badge.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Submission of a PDF report detailing the exam findings.

## Who Should Take CRT-ID?

The CRT-ID certification is ideal for:

- Red team operators looking to enhance their infrastructure skills.
- Penetration testers interested in developing robust attack environments.
- Cybersecurity consultants aiming to broaden their expertise in offensive security.
