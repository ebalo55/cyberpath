## Overview

The **Certified Blue Team Fundamentals (BTF)** certification is designed for individuals looking to start their journey
in cyber defense. This certification provides foundational knowledge in security operations, threat hunting, incident
response, and digital forensics, making it ideal for beginners.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Understand the roles and responsibilities of cyber defenders.
- Analyze various types of cyber attacks using defensive solutions.
- Implement incident response strategies and conduct digital investigations.

## Course Details

The BTF certification consists of seven comprehensive sections, each focusing on different aspects of blue team
operations.

### Topics Covered:

- **Introduction to Cyber Operations**: Overview of cybersecurity principles and practices.
- **Foundations of Cyber Threat Analysis and Intelligence**: Understanding threat landscapes and intelligence gathering.
- **Proactive Cyber Threat Hunting**: Techniques for identifying potential threats before they materialize.
- **Incident Response Strategies and Techniques**: Best practices for responding to security incidents.
- **Digital Investigations**: Methods for conducting thorough investigations post-incident.
- **Lab Exercise**: Practical application through a guided lab setup.

## Exam Structure

The BTF certification is awarded upon successful completion of a quiz-based exam that allows unlimited attempts.
Candidates are encouraged to engage with the course materials and practical exercises to prepare effectively.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Participation in interactive quizzes designed to reinforce learning.

## Who Should Take BTF?

The BTF certification is ideal for:

- Beginners interested in pursuing a career in cybersecurity defense.
- Individuals seeking to understand the fundamentals of blue team operations.
- Cybersecurity managers wanting to enhance their team's foundational skills.
