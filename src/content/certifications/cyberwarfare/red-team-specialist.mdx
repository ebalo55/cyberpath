## Overview

The **Certified Red Team Specialist (CRTS)** certification is designed for professionals seeking to deepen their
knowledge and skills in red teaming and advanced cyber attack simulations. This certification equips candidates with the
tools and techniques necessary to effectively simulate attacks on enterprise environments.

## Course Details

The CRTS certification is structured into four comprehensive sections, each focusing on different aspects of red team
operations.

### Topics Covered:

- **Attack Simulation**: Techniques for simulating real-world attacks.
- **Abusing MS**: Strategies for exploiting Microsoft technologies.
- **Lateral Movement**: Methods for moving within networks after initial access.
- **Abuse Enterprise Grade Software**: Understanding vulnerabilities in enterprise applications.

## Exam Structure

The CRTS certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that tests candidates on their ability
to perform red team operations in a controlled environment. Candidates will engage in realistic scenarios that require
both technical skills and strategic thinking.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Demonstration of practical skills in a lab setting.

## Who Should Take CRTS?

The CRTS certification is ideal for:

- Intermediate to advanced cybersecurity professionals looking to specialize in red teaming.
- Penetration testers seeking to enhance their offensive security capabilities.
- Threat hunters wanting to understand attacker methodologies better.
