## Overview

The **Certified Purple Team Analyst (CPTA)** certification is designed for cybersecurity professionals who aim to bridge
the gap between offensive and defensive security practices. This certification equips candidates with the skills
necessary to collaborate effectively within purple teams, enhancing both red and blue team operations.

- Implement offensive security techniques alongside defensive strategies.
- Utilize various security tools and methodologies for effective security testing.
- Conduct comprehensive assessments that improve organizational security posture.

## Course Details

The CPTA certification is organized into five comprehensive sections, each focusing on essential aspects of purple
teaming.

### Topics Covered:

- **Introduction to Purple Teaming**: Understanding the collaborative nature of red and blue teams.
- **Offensive Security Techniques**: Exploring tactics used in penetration testing and red teaming.
- **Defensive Security Strategies**: Learning how to counteract offensive techniques effectively.
- **Defensive Security Tools and Techniques**: Familiarization with tools that support defensive operations.
- **Security Testing Methodologies**: Best practices for conducting thorough security assessments.

## Exam Structure

The CPTA certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that assesses the candidate's ability
to apply learned techniques in realistic scenarios.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Participation in hands-on labs that simulate real-world purple team engagements.

## Who Should Take CPTA?

The CPTA certification is ideal for:

- Intermediate-level cybersecurity professionals seeking to enhance their skills in both offensive and defensive
  tactics.
- Red teamers wanting to understand blue team methodologies for better collaboration.
- Blue teamers looking to gain insights into red team strategies to improve their defense mechanisms.
