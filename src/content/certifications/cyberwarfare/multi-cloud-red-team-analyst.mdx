## Overview

The **Certified Multi-Cloud Red Team Analyst (MCRTA)** certification is designed for cybersecurity professionals who
want to specialize in red teaming across multiple cloud environments. This certification provides the skills necessary
to conduct effective penetration tests and security assessments in AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud platforms.

- Execute red team operations in multi-cloud environments.
- Identify vulnerabilities and exploit misconfigurations in cloud services.
- Develop strategies for effective cloud security assessments.

## Course Details

The MCRTA certification includes four comprehensive sections, each focusing on specific aspects of red teaming in cloud
environments.

### Topics Covered:

- **Introduction to Multi-Cloud Red Team**: Overview of red teaming principles and practices in cloud settings.
- **Red Teaming in AWS Cloud**: Techniques and tools for conducting assessments within AWS.
- **Red Teaming in Azure Cloud**: Strategies for exploiting vulnerabilities specific to Microsoft Azure.
- **Red Teaming in Google Cloud**: Approaches for identifying and mitigating risks in Google Cloud environments.

## Exam Structure

The MCRTA certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that assesses the candidate's ability
to perform red team operations across different cloud platforms.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all course modules.
- Participation in hands-on labs that simulate real-world cloud environments.

## Who Should Take MCRTA?

The MCRTA certification is ideal for:

- Intermediate to advanced cybersecurity professionals looking to enhance their skills in cloud security.
- Red teamers wanting to expand their expertise into multi-cloud environments.
- Penetration testers seeking to understand the unique challenges posed by cloud infrastructures.
