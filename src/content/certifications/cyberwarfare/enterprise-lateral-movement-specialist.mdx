## Overview

The **Certified Enterprise Lateral Movement Specialist (CELMS)** certification focuses on understanding and executing
lateral movement techniques within networks. This certification is designed for individuals seeking to enhance their
skills in simulating real-world attacks and understanding attacker strategies.

- Understand and exploit various authentication mechanisms.
- Utilize remote management protocols effectively.
- Simulate lateral movement scenarios in a controlled environment.

## Course Details

The CELMS certification includes a comprehensive course that covers both external and internal lateral movement tactics.
Candidates will engage in hands-on exercises and simulations to develop practical skills.

### Topics Covered:

- Protocols such as WMI, SMB, RPC, and SSH.
- Tools for executing lateral movement over different protocols.
- Techniques for abusing Kerberos tickets and delegation.
- Realistic scenarios for credential harvesting within networks.

## Exam Structure

The CELMS certification is awarded upon successful completion of a **24-hour practical exam**, where candidates must
demonstrate their understanding of lateral movement techniques through a series of challenges. A detailed report
submission is required post-exam.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- 24-hour exam window focusing on practical application.
- Minimum passing score of **85%** to earn the Accredible Badge.

## Who Should Take CELMS?

The CELMS certification is ideal for:

- Aspiring penetration testers looking to specialize in lateral movement techniques.
- Red team specialists aiming to refine their adversarial simulation skills.
- Security operations professionals wanting to understand attacker methodologies.
