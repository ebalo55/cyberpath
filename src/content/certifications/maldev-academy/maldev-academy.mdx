The **Certified Malware Development Specialist** certification offered by Maldev Academy is designed for

## Course Details

The course consists of a series of modules that cover essential topics in malware development.

### Topics Covered:

- **Process Injection**: Techniques for injecting code into other processes.
- **Payload Execution Control**: Managing how payloads are executed within target environments.
- **IAT Hiding & Obfuscation**: Methods for hiding import address tables and obfuscating code.
- **API Hooking**: Understanding how to hook APIs for malicious purposes.
- **Static Evasion Techniques**: Strategies for avoiding detection by static analysis tools.
- **Anti-Debugging Techniques**: Implementing measures to prevent debugging of malware.
- **Payload Staging and Compilation**: Techniques for staging payloads and compiling them effectively.

### Learning Materials:

Participants will have access to a wealth of resources, including video tutorials, practical exercises, and community
support, designed to enhance their learning experience.

## Exam Structure

The certification is awarded upon successful completion of all course modules. There is no formal exam; candidates
demonstrate their understanding through practical application within the course.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all modules in the course.
- Participation in practical exercises designed to reinforce learning.
