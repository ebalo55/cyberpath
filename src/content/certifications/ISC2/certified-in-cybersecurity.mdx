## Overview

The **Certified in Cybersecurity (CC)** certification is an entry-level credential offered by ISC2, designed to validate
foundational knowledge and skills necessary for a career in cybersecurity. This certification is ideal for individuals
looking to start their journey in the cybersecurity field without requiring prior experience.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Understand and apply security principles and practices.
- Implement basic access control measures.
- Recognize and respond to security incidents.

## Course Details

The CC certification consists of comprehensive training that covers essential cybersecurity topics.

### Topics Covered:

- **Security Principles**: Fundamental concepts of security and risk management.
- **Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery**: Understanding the importance of continuity planning.
- **Access Control Concepts**: Implementing effective access control measures.
- **Network Security**: Basic principles of securing networks against threats.
- **Security Operations**: Overview of security operations and incident response.

### Learning Materials:

Participants will have access to a variety of resources, including online self-paced training, practice quizzes, and
study guides designed to prepare them for the certification exam.

## Exam Structure

The CC certification is awarded upon successful completion of the exam, which assesses knowledge across the covered
domains.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- No prior work experience required.
- Free exam and training available for a limited time.

## Who Should Take CC?

The CC certification is ideal for:

- IT professionals looking to transition into cybersecurity roles.
- College students or recent graduates aiming to start a career in cybersecurity.
- Individuals seeking to validate their foundational cybersecurity knowledge.
