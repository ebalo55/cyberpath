## Overview

The **Certified Cloud Security Professional (CCSP)** certification is a globally recognized credential offered by ISC2,
designed for experienced IT and cybersecurity professionals who have a deep understanding of cloud computing. This
certification validates an individual's ability to design, manage, and secure data, applications, and infrastructure in
the cloud using best practices, policies, and procedures.

The certification validates an individual's ability to:

- Apply cloud security principles and best practices.
- Manage and protect data, applications, and infrastructure in the cloud.
- Implement effective risk management strategies for cloud environments.

## Course Details

The CCSP certification covers six domains that encompass essential aspects of cloud security.

### Topics Covered:

- **Cloud Concepts, Architecture and Design**: Understanding cloud computing concepts and architectures.
- **Cloud Data Security**: Protecting data in the cloud throughout its lifecycle.
- **Cloud Platform and Infrastructure Security**: Securing cloud platforms and underlying infrastructure.
- **Cloud Application Security**: Implementing security measures for cloud-based applications.
- **Cloud Security Operations**: Managing security operations in the cloud.
- **Legal, Risk and Compliance**: Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements in the cloud.

### Learning Materials:

Participants will have access to a variety of resources, including official training materials, practice exams, and
community support from cloud security professionals.

## Exam Structure

The CCSP certification is awarded upon successful completion of the exam that assesses knowledge across the covered
domains.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- A minimum of five years of cumulative paid work experience in one or more of the six domains of the CCSP Common Body
  of Knowledge (CBK).
- The exam consists of multiple-choice questions covering all domains.

## Who Should Take CCSP?

The CCSP certification is ideal for:

- Cloud security specialists responsible for designing, implementing, and managing cloud security solutions.
- Cybersecurity managers looking to validate their expertise in cloud security governance and risk management.
- Risk management specialists aiming to enhance their understanding of cloud-specific risks and mitigation strategies.
