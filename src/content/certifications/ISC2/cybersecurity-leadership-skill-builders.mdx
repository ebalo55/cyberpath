## Overview

The **ISC2 Cybersecurity Leadership Skill-Builders (CSLB)** are designed to help cybersecurity professionals excel in
their careers by staying current with the latest trends and best practices. These on-demand learning modules, created by
industry experts, provide key perspectives on the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity and their real-world
applications for executive- and board-level planning and decision-making.

The certification validates an individual's ability to:

- Develop effective cybersecurity strategies.
- Communicate cyber risks to stakeholders.
- Lead teams and manage cybersecurity operations.

## Course Details

The CSLB bundle includes several key modules:

1. **The What, Why, Who and How of Cybersecurity Strategy**: This 15-minute module explores the elements of a
   cybersecurity strategy, its importance, key stakeholders, and the process of creating and implementing it[1].

2. **Budget and Talent Shortfalls**: This 15-minute module covers how cybersecurity leaders determine budgets, allocate
   funds, and ensure they have enough skilled professionals to protect their organization's assets[1].

3. **Cyber Ethics Overview**: This 15-minute module examines trending cybersecurity ethics considerations and what
   should be included in an effective code of ethics[1].

4. **Cybersecurity Strategies**: This 15-minute module looks at strategies used to mitigate risks, protect sensitive
   data, systems, and networks, and how to use existing security benchmarks and compliance standards to design a
   comprehensive cybersecurity strategy[1].

5. **Developing an API Security Strategy**: This 15-minute module explores the importance of securing APIs and
   strategies for reducing risks and vulnerabilities associated with these interfaces[1].

### Key Benefits:

- Stay current with emerging cybersecurity leadership topics.
- Receive quick, on-demand learning that fits your busy schedule.
- Earn CPE credits for maintaining your ISC2 certifications.

## Exam Structure

The CSLB modules do not have a formal exam structure. Learners complete the modules at their own pace and earn CPE
credits upon successful completion.

## Who Should Take CSLB?

The CSLB is ideal for:

- Cybersecurity managers looking to enhance their leadership skills.
- Risk management specialists aiming to communicate cyber risks effectively.
- Security operations specialists focused on leading cybersecurity teams.
