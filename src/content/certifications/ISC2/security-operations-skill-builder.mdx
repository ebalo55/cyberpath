## Overview

The **ISC2 Security Operations Skill-Builders (SOSB)** are designed to enhance the capabilities of cybersecurity
professionals in managing security operations effectively. These on-demand courses provide essential insights into
threat detection, incident response, and the latest trends in security operations.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Implement effective security operations strategies.
- Manage and respond to security incidents.
- Utilize tools and techniques for threat detection and mitigation.

## Course Details

The SOSB bundle includes several key modules:

1. **Preventing and Mitigating API Data Breaches**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 15 Minutes
    - **CPE**: 0.50
    - **Proficiency Level**: Foundational
    - This course explores common threats and vulnerabilities that can expose APIs to attacks, along with strategies to
      mitigate these risks.

2. **Data Protection: Classification, Categorization and Security of Data**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 1 Hour
    - **CPE**: 1
    - **Proficiency Level**: Foundational
    - Learn about data classification practices and how to enhance overall data security within your organization.

3. **Cybersecurity in Industrial Control Systems (ICS)**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 2 Hours
    - **CPE**: 2
    - **Proficiency Level**: Foundational
    - This course covers the unique security challenges associated with ICS environments and how to address them
      effectively.

4. **Building a Strong Culture of Security: Foundations of Engaging SETA**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 1 Hour
    - **CPE**: 1
    - **Proficiency Level**: Foundational
    - Discover how to create an effective Security Education, Training, and Awareness (SETA) program within your
      organization.

5. **Security in the IoT Ecosystem**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 1 Hour
    - **CPE**: 1
    - **Proficiency Level**: Intermediate
    - Understand the security challenges posed by the Internet of Things (IoT) and how to protect sensitive data in IoT
      deployments.

### Key Benefits:

- Stay current with emerging security operations topics.
- Quick, on-demand learning that fits your busy schedule.
- Earn CPE credits for maintaining your ISC2 certifications.

## Exam Structure

The SOSB modules do not have a formal exam structure. Participants complete the modules at their own pace and earn CPE
credits upon successful completion.

## Who Should Take SOSB?

The SOSB is ideal for:

- Cybersecurity managers looking to enhance their operational skills.
- Security operations specialists focused on incident response and threat management.
- Risk management specialists aiming to improve their organization's security posture.
