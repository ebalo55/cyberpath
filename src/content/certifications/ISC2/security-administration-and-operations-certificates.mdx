## Overview

The **ISC2 Security Administration and Operations Certificates (SAOC)** are designed to equip cybersecurity
professionals with the essential skills needed to install, administer, and troubleshoot security solutions effectively.
These certificates provide a pathway to enhance your expertise in security operations while preparing you for the SSCP
certification.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Design and implement incident response plans.
- Analyze and respond to malware threats.
- Develop and manage crowdsourced security programs.

## Course Details

The SAOC bundle includes several key courses:

1. **Responding to a Breach**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 4 Hours
    - **CPE**: 4
    - **Proficiency Level**: Intermediate
    - This course focuses on designing incident response plans and applying frameworks to various cyber incidents.

2. **Malware Analysis**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 2 Hours
    - **CPE**: 2
    - **Proficiency Level**: Intermediate
    - Learn how to find, analyze, and interpret malware samples, along with techniques used to evade detection.

3. **Crowdsourced Security**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 2 Hours
    - **CPE**: 2
    - **Proficiency Level**: Intermediate
    - This course covers developing a crowd-enabled security program that integrates collective intelligence into your
      security strategy.

4. **Cryptography**
    - **Delivery Method**: On-Demand
    - **Duration**: 4 Hours
    - **CPE**: 4
    - **Proficiency Level**: Foundational
    - Understand the fundamental concepts and applications of modern cryptography to protect sensitive information.

### Key Benefits:

- Act as a pathway to the ISC2 SSCP certification.
- Demonstrate commitment to maintaining your ISC2 Certification through professional development.
- Stay ahead of the curve by gaining expertise in emerging cybersecurity topics.
- Earn CPE credits for maintaining your certifications.

## Exam Structure

To earn each SAOC certificate, candidates must score 70% or higher on the final assessment. Participants can choose to
complete the full course or go directly to the assessment if they already possess the necessary knowledge.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Passing score of 70% or higher on the final assessment.
- Option to complete learning modules or take assessment only.

## Who Should Take SAOC?

The SAOC is ideal for:

- Cybersecurity managers looking to enhance their operational skills.
- Security operations specialists focused on incident response and malware analysis.
- Risk management specialists aiming to integrate crowdsourced security into their strategies.
