## Overview

The **MSAF - System Administration Fundamentals** certification is designed for individuals seeking to establish a solid
foundation in IT system administration. This certification covers essential skills necessary for managing and optimizing
various IT systems, including network infrastructure, servers, and applications.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Plan, install, configure, and maintain IT systems.
- Support and optimize network infrastructures and applications.
- Understand the basics of cybersecurity as it relates to system administration.

## Course Details

The MSAF certification program provides comprehensive training in system administration, making it an excellent starting
point for those new to the field. Participants will learn practical skills that are crucial for a successful career in
cybersecurity.

### Topics Covered:

- Overview of IT system administration processes.
- Configuration and management of servers and network devices.
- Maintenance of desktops, laptops, and applications.
- Introduction to cybersecurity principles relevant to system administration.

## Exam Structure

The MSAF certification is awarded upon successful completion of a practical exam that assesses the candidate's ability
to apply system administration skills in real-world scenarios.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Practical exercises simulating typical system administration tasks.
- Documentation of procedures followed during the exam.

## Who Should Take MSAF?

The MSAF certification is ideal for:

- Aspiring cybersecurity managers looking to build foundational IT skills.
- Security operations specialists aiming to enhance their understanding of system management.
- Cloud security specialists who need a solid grounding in system administration practices.
