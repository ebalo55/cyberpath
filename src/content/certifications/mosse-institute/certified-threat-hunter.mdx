## Overview

The **MTH Certified Threat Hunter** certification is designed for cybersecurity professionals aiming to excel in
proactive threat detection and mitigation. Offered by the MCSI, this globally-recognized credential equips candidates
with the skills necessary to identify and address threats before they can cause significant damage.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Execute threat hunting engagements in large-scale networks.
- Identify and target threats early to enhance security posture.
- Utilize advanced tools and techniques for detecting malicious activities.

## Course Details

The MTH certification focuses on mastering the art and science of threat hunting. It emphasizes a deep understanding of
network environments and the proactive measures necessary to prevent data breaches.

### Topics Covered:

- Fundamentals of threat hunting methodologies.
- Techniques for identifying malicious activity within networks.
- Tools and technologies used in threat detection.
- Best practices for improving organizational security posture.

## Exam Structure

Candidates must complete a comprehensive assessment that tests their knowledge and practical skills in threat hunting.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Evaluation of threat detection scenarios.
- Demonstration of effective hunting techniques.
- Documentation of findings and methodologies used during the assessment.

## Who Should Take MTH?

The MTH certification is ideal for:

- Aspiring Threat Hunters looking to enhance their detection skills.
- Blue Team Specialists aiming to improve their incident response capabilities.
- Security Operations Specialists focused on proactive security measures.
