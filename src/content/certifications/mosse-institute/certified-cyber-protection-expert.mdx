## Overview

The **MCPE Certified Cyber Protection Expert** certification is designed for professionals aiming to excel in defensive
cyber operations. Offered by the MCSI, this certification equips candidates with the skills necessary to effectively
protect networks and respond to cyber threats.

The certification validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Characterize cyber terrain and identify concealed threats.
- Assess risks and perform forensic analysis.
- Respond to intrusions and recover compromised networks.

## Course Details

The MCPE certification is tied to a comprehensive training program that focuses on the DoD Cyber Protection Team (CPT)
workflow. Participants will engage in hands-on learning experiences that prepare them for real-world cyber defense
scenarios.

### Topics Covered:

- Cyber terrain characterization and threat identification.
- Risk assessment methodologies.
- Forensics and malware analysis techniques.
- Incident response strategies and network recovery.

## Exam Structure

Candidates must complete a thorough assessment that evaluates their knowledge and practical skills in cyber protection.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Evaluation of cyber defense scenarios.
- Demonstration of forensic analysis and incident response techniques.
- Documentation of methodologies and findings.

## Who Should Take MCPE?

The MCPE certification is ideal for:

- Aspiring Blue Team Specialists focused on defensive operations.
- Cybersecurity Managers looking to enhance their team's capabilities in threat response.
- Security Operations Specialists aiming to improve their operational readiness against cyber threats.
