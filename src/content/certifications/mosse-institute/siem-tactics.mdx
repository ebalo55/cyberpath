## Overview

The **SIEM Tactics Bootcamp** is an intensive training program designed for professionals seeking to enhance their
skills in threat detection and incident response using Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) systems. This
20-hour course equips participants with the knowledge necessary to defend against sophisticated cyber threats
effectively.

The bootcamp validates an individualâ€™s ability to:

- Analyze and respond to security incidents using a SIEM platform.
- Identify and investigate potential threats in real-time.
- Assess risks and implement effective security measures.

## Course Details

The SIEM Tactics Bootcamp focuses on practical, hands-on training, enabling participants to gain experience in using
SIEM tools for threat detection and hunting.

### Topics Covered:

- Fundamentals of SIEM systems and their role in cybersecurity.
- Techniques for threat detection and incident investigation.
- Best practices for configuring and managing SIEM platforms.
- Real-world scenarios for responding to security incidents.

## Exam Structure

While the bootcamp does not have a formal exam, participants will engage in practical exercises that simulate real-world
incident response situations, allowing them to apply what they have learned.

### Key Training Requirements:

- Active participation in hands-on labs and exercises.
- Collaboration with peers to solve complex security challenges.

## Who Should Take SIEM Tactics?

The SIEM Tactics Bootcamp is ideal for:

- Blue team specialists looking to enhance their threat detection capabilities.
- Cybersecurity managers aiming to improve their team's incident response strategies.
- Security operations specialists focused on defending against cyber threats.
