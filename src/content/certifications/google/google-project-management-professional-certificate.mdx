## Overview

The **Google Project Management: Professional Certificate (GPMPC)** is designed to fast-track your career in project
management. Offered by Google through Coursera, this certificate program provides in-demand skills and AI training from
Google experts.

## Course Details

The GPMPC is a 6-course series that covers the essential skills needed for an entry-level project management role. The
program is self-paced and includes over 140 hours of instruction and practice-based assessments.

### Topics Covered:

- Foundations of project management.
- Project initiation, planning, and execution.
- Agile project management with a focus on Scrum.
- Risk management and quality management.

## Exam Structure

The certification is awarded upon successful completion of all six courses, which include hands-on projects and
assessments designed to simulate real-world scenarios.

### Key Exam Requirements:

- Completion of all six courses.
- Submission of portfolio-ready projects demonstrating proficiency.

## Who Should Take GPMPC?

The GPMPC is ideal for:

- Aspiring project managers.
- Professionals looking to transition into project management roles.
- Individuals seeking to enhance their project management skills with a focus on Agile methodologies.
